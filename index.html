About Us



Swole Sisters is a New York based women's fitness club founded by 2 sisters, Ariana and Sam.

Ariana and Sam began their fitness journey in 2016. They both fell in love with weightlifting, but they soon realized that a lot of women didn't feel comfortable working out in crowded gym settings.

In an effort to encourage other women to feel confident in the gym, Ariana and Sam created Swole Sisters - a fitness club that is open to women of all ages and fitness levels. The club hosts 3 workouts per week, which can include weightlifting, cardio circuits, Pilates, and Yoga. Swole Sisters is partnered with several gyms across New York, and plans to expand even further!
